BEER IS GOOD and FUN TO EXPLORE.

BEERxMATCH is a web application that visualizes personal beer check-in data from an iPhone/Android app Untappd.

With Untappd, you can check in beers you drink: search your beer and rate it up to 5 stars. Of course, when you check in, timestamp is also saved. While checking in, you can add tag a location where you drink.Untappd also provides meta data of each beer - style, brewery, location of brewery, and ABV.

BEYOND LOGGING.
VISUALIZE YOUR BEERS and EVEN FRIENDS'.

Beer Match utilizes these fantastic data and visualizes your beer taste and drinking behaviors and patterns. You'll see how much and often you drink, what styles or breweries you like, your favorite beers, and when and where you drink. With these visualizations.

In addition to the visualization of a single Untappd user, Beer Match compares two users. Analyzing two users' beer taste, it shows the match score (100% is the soul mate of beer) and compares their drinking behavior and many more from many aspects.
